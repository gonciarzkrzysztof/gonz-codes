Ruby on Rails Developer at Netguru


Over the past eight years we have developed nearly 160 projects for clients from five different timezones, and we don’t intend to slow down. Join our team as Ruby on Rails Developer and create highest-quality code with us.
About RoR Team
Our Ruby on Rails crew consists of more than 80 people, and we are proud to call ourselves Ruby on Rails consulting experts.
Tools we use

We use various tools and methodologies that allow us to be a fully-remote software house and do our work efficiently and successfully at the same time. Among other things we rely on:

  • Jira as an Issue Tracking System
  • Confluence as a knowledge repository
  • Scrum/Agile as a project management methodology
  • GitHub for code reviews
  • Codebeat for static code analysis
  • Slack for internal communication
What to expect?
We believe that quality is the key to success and that’s why we do our best to deliver as clean and well-written code as possible. You’ll be part of a team that consists of Frontend Developers, Product Designers, Quality Assurance Specialists and a Project Manager. As a Ruby on Rails Developer, you'll be a crucial member of this team, and you’ll have a chance to contribute to applications we create for start-up type clients from different regions – mostly the UK, the US and Asia.
